Getting Started

Ping sends a text message to another phone. The other phone responds with its location. For this to work, Ping must be installed on both phones. To do this:

  1. Install Ping on your phone.
  2. Install Ping on the other phone.
  3. Start Ping on both phones.
  4. Click "Start Service" on both phones.
  5. Click "Choose a Phone to Ping" on your phone.
  6. Choose the other phone. This will send a text.
  7. The other phone will show a prompt "Allow Ping Request?". Click "Yes".

The other phone number will now be added to your "white-list" of contacts who can ping you.

Ping will also send text message reply with the location (latitude and longitude) of your phone. This location will be shown by a marker on the map on the other phone.

Ping Version: 6
